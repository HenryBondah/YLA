Henry Dean Bondah

A passionate young leader on a mission to inspire individuals to push their limits and contribute to global betterment. He values the uniqueness of each person's potential, which fuels his commitment to fostering positive change. Currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering and a minor in Finance at Brigham Young University Idaho (BYUl), Henry's enthusiasm for business and technology is complemented by his role as the founder and CEO of ScREAte, overseeing ventures like ScREAte Media and ScREAte Tech. He also manages businesses in West Africa and works on projects like ScREAte Farms.

Driven by ambition and motivation, Henry believes in the power of positivity. He utilizes various tools to connect with young people, encouraging them to be problem solvers and solutions seekers. Through ScREAte Media, he hosts motivational podcasts and shares uplifting news, aiming to make people smile even in tough times.

Drawing from diverse life experiences, Henry aspires to empower every young adult to realize their full potential. He strongly believes in our shared humanity and the ability of individuals to enhance the world. He urges us to embrace our uniqueness and use it for the betterment of the Earth, emphasizing the importance of support for his cause. "Our uniqueness may be opaque sometimes, but when it becomes clear, let's make good use of it for the sake of the Earth..
