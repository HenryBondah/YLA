What We Offer

Established in 2020, Young Leader has quickly become a beloved platform for African youth, both on the continent and in the diaspora. It serves as a vital space where young individuals can confidently exhibit and articulate their strengths, potentials, skills, talents, ideas, visions, and experiences. Their collective goal is to play an active role in advancing Africa's development and asserting its autonomy in the global arena.
Young Leaders of Africa is especially noteworthy for its annual forum, a highly anticipated event. Each year, this forum converges in a different city chosen from various locations across Africa. This geographical diversity allows the forum to harness the richness of Africa's culture, resources, and ideas.
Through this annual gathering, Young Leader cultivates a sense of unity, enabling young leaders to come together and engage in discussions and activities that contribute to the growth and progress of the African continent. The forum provides a unique opportunity for youth to collaborate, share innovative ideas, and build lasting connections with their peers, fostering the spirit of collective empowerment and self-determination in Africa's future.
