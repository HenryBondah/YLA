Alvins Kabuya

He is widely recognized for his creative and ideological abilities.Alvins is a dynamic entrepreneur who has left an enduring mark in the business world. He founded Global Promo Corporation in 2018, a notable marketing and media communication agency showcasing his keen understanding of the industry's dynamics. In 2020, Alvins extended his impact by establishing the Global Support Foundation, a non-profit focusing on humanitarian aid, embodying his commitment to social responsibility.

Passionate about youth empowerment, Alvins founded Young Leader, a Pan-African forum dedicated to nurturing entrepreneurship and leadership among the younger generation. Serving as CEO of "LEADERS & LEGENDS" magazine, he captures the essence of leadership and highlights impactful individuals.

Beyond business success, Alvins distinguishes himself as a socially conscious entrepreneur. His journey, marked by determination and persistence, demonstrates his ability to navigate various sectors with unwavering focus. Alvins, the young Congolese entrepreneur, has not only made a mark in the business landscape but also showcased a profound commitment to positive societal impact
