Who We Are

Pan-African forum of young leaders is a significant platform where young people from various African countries come together to discuss, collaborate, and address pressing issues concerning their nations and the continent as a whole. This forum typically focus on topics such as entrepreneurship, leadership, sustainable development, social innovation, and political engagement.
Participants in this forum often include young entrepreneurs, activists, scholars, and professionals who share a common interest in driving positive change in Africa. Through panel discussions, workshops, networking sessions, and collaborative projects, attendees exchange ideas, learn from one another, and develop strategies to tackle challenges faced by African youth.
This forum also provide a unique opportunity for young leaders to connect with established figures in politics, business, and civil society, enabling mentorship and fostering partnerships that can contribute to the overall development of the African continent. By promoting collaboration and empowering young leaders, these forums play a crucial role in shaping the future of Africa and nurturing the next generation of African leaders.
